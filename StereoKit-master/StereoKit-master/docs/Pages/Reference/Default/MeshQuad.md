## Description
A default quad mesh, 2 triangles, 4 verts, from
(-0.5,-0.5,0) to (0.5,0.5,0) and facing forward on the Z axis
(0,0,-1). White vertex colors, and UVs from (1,1) at vertex
(-0.5,-0.5,0) to (0,0) at vertex (0.5,0.5,0).
